Award Value

One (1) $1000 non-renewable scholarship.

Deadline

December 30, 2019 All application materials must be submitted to scholarship@omegalul.org by midnight PDT on December 30. Late or incomplete applications not eligible for current or future awards.

Eligibility

Open to all majors and entering freshman, undergraduate and graduate students currently enrolled full time in an accredited US college.

Application Materials

1) 500-600 WORD ESSAY: Describe your passion for entrepreneurship and any future plans, including your volunteer work, hobbies, and professional or academic involvement. Include any relevant links at the end of the essay.
2) College transcript (unofficial)

Requirements

One submission per applicant. Award amount will be made out and sent to applicant's home address.

Process

The scholarship winner will be selected by Omegalul scholarship committee. Omegalul will inform the winner via the phone number or email address provided in the scholarship application. If the winner does not reply by phone or email within 96 hours after being contacted by Omegalul, a new winner will be selected. Late or incomplete submissions, along with application materials that do not meet all of the stated requirements are not eligible for consideration.
